Important AICTE Instruction

  • LoA/EoA letters (since inception) must be part of the mandatory disclosure.
  • Complete mandatory disclosure should be converted into a single PDF and URL entered in AICTE portal attachments tab.
  • The disclosure must be freely viewable/downloadable without restrictions.
  • The website should be dynamically updated with latest mandatory disclosure information.
  • Suppression or misrepresentation of information may invite penal action.
