Eligibility

UG programmes require a minimum of 50% aggregate in qualifying examination (10+2 or equivalent). PG programmes require a relevant Bachelor's degree with a minimum 50% aggregate.

Admission Process

Admissions are conducted through the state-level common entrance process. Merit-based selection is followed. Management quota seats are filled as per state government norms.

Affiliation & Approval

All programmes are affiliated to the State University and approved by AICTE. The college is recognised by the UGC under Section 2(f) and 12(B).
